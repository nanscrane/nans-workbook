What is P5.js?

A great example of creative drawing using p5.js: Week 4 Homework - Create a digital portrait for your classmates.
The portrait is entirely composed of geometric shapes. Some curved structures such as eyebrows are also created by overlapping and drawing between shapes.

This section explores what P5.js is and learns how to create a canvas in processing. Later, we learn to create various geometric shapes on this canvas and fill them with color. there is some example:
circle(x,y,size)
ellipse(x,y,w,h)
square(x,y,size)
rect(x,y,w,h)
line(x1,y1,x2,y2)

A Throw Of The Dice

Some sketches demonstrating dice rolling game.

Here's the prototype of the dice rolling game we played on paper. We established some rules, such as rolling a six-sided die produces a circle, while an eight-sided die produces a square. When two colors match, they form a golden triangle, and three matches create a flower.
