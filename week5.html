WEEk 5 activities

Make a pointillism picture of your photograph

We can also draw videos on the brush by using createCapture(VIDEO) combined with the camera. The brush will capture the video from your camera and turn it into frames of photos, which helps us create videos that can be painted.

This week we learned how to import photos and camera videos for drawing. We learned to use photos and videos as painting brushes on the p5 canvas. At the same time, we also tried using MousePressed to switch between different photos/filters to create brush strokes on the canvas.
